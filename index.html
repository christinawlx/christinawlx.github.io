About ME

Hi, I am Lingxiao Wang, majoring in Interactive Media and now pursuing my MFA degree at USC. I was majoring in Digital Media Technology(Game Design Deriction) at Communication University of China when I was an undergraduate, and made various type of games(pc games, mobile games, VR game, games involve localnetwork and reaching an online database) using different tools. In the past I developed games mainly as programmer and designer, but now I also begin to learn art tools such as Maya.

I really enjoying video editing in my spare time. On one hand, I like the feeling of matching images with rythems.On the other hand, it is fun to break the existed plots and ressemble them to a new story(such as what would happen if characters from two different animations meet). You can see those videos in my Youtube channel.

WHERE IS MY PRINCESS

Program/Mechenic Desgin/Puzzle Design

Where Is My Princess is a 3D platform game made with Unity. In the game, players control the protagonist, a "frog", to get over difficulties by using laser to connect to fireflies, in order to collect seven game cartridges and finally meet his prinecess.

bREATH

Program/Project Management

In the game Breath, players take an adventure in a foggy, ruined city full of monsters, with only an air gun in their hind. In order to survive in the tough environment, players need to consider how to spend their limited oxygen - supplying breath, making air wall, or flying. The game was made with Unity.

Lost in eden

Program/UI Effect Desgin(Old Version)   Puzzle Design(New Version)

Lost In Eden is a first person puzzle game made with UE4, where players use their special ability "flashback" to solve puzzles and finally figure out what happened  in the past space station, Eden. When players are "flashingback", they do not have collision with other objects and therefore will not trig swithes, which is the base of all the puzzles.

I quit this project in order to focus on my bachelor thesis. After that new members joined and the project's  name was changed to Cyborg Lab. It is a cool project with 20+ puzzles. In this new project,  I designed some puzzles and also edit this trailer.
